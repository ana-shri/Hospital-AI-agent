Hospital Admission Documents:
- Government ID proof
- Health insurance card
- Doctor referral letter
- Previous medical reports
- List of current medications

Before Surgery:
- Wear comfortable clothing
- Do not eat or drink if instructed by doctor
- Carry personal hygiene items
- Bring required medications
- Carry insurance and ID documents

Discharge Process:
- Doctor reviews recovery
- Final tests may be performed
- Payment and insurance clearance
- Discharge summary is provided
- Instructions for medications are given

Follow-ups After Hospitalisation:
- Follow-up doctor visit within 7–14 days
- Wound care check
- Medication review
- Possible lab tests
- Lifestyle and recovery instructions
